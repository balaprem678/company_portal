Our Story

Pillai’s Foods is a family-owned business, ventured by us to share with you the incredible, healthy foods that we have experienced eating from our childhood in India.

We the descendants of VSM & KP Pillai’s, bring you purest, natural, wholesome foods with all the nutrients intact in each grain, directly from the small family farms across India to your kitchen.

We remember with such fondness our childhood memories on family farms in our ancestral homes, where the rice on our table came from our own paddy fields, the flour milled at home by our grandmothers and aunts, the vegetables grown in abundance right in our backyard to name a few.

All our grains are predominantly unpolished as it always used to be, holding on to the healthy goodness in them, our cooking oils are cold-pressed by us, traditionally how we used to watch being ground and extracted at a bull-driven stone / wood oil press, down the street.

Our healthy instant mixes, masalas, ground spices, instant noodles, pasta, pickles to anything we offer has no artificial colours or preservatives in them which is unique compared to the rest in the market.

Now at Pillai’s, we thought to share the same experience, pleasure and goodness we experienced growing up, with you.

We’ve put an immense amount of passion into each of our products and we hope you love them as much as we do!!!

Come join us and serve the BEST meal to your family & friends. “Making healthy eating a part of your lifestyle”
